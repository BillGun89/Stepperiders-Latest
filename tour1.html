Desert & Mountain Tour

Experience the breathtaking landscapes of Mongolia from desert dunes to mountain lakes

Tour Overview

Our Desert & Mountain Tour takes you on an unforgettable journey through the diverse landscapes of Mongolia. From the vast steppes near Ulaanbaatar to the crystal-clear waters of Khuvsgul Lake and the breathtaking mountain scenery, this 9-day adventure showcases the best of Mongolia's natural beauty.

Ride through changing terrains, experience the nomadic lifestyle, and discover hidden gems that few tourists ever see. This tour is perfect for riders looking for a balance of challenge and comfort, with a total distance of approximately 1,790 kilometers covered over 9 days.

Total Distance

1,790 km

Duration

9 Days

Terrain

Steppe, Mountain, Forest

Accommodation

Ger Camps & Hotels

Our experienced local guides will lead you through remote areas while our support team ensures your comfort and safety. All meals, accommodations, and motorcycle rentals are included in this all-inclusive adventure package.

Day by Day Itinerary

1/9
1

Ulaanbaatar ‚Üí Elsen Tasarkhai ‚Üí Tsetserleg

270 km

We begin our journey early in the morning, leaving Ulaanbaatar behind and heading west. Our first major stop is at the stunning Elsen Tasarkhai sand dunes, a miniature Gobi in the middle of the steppe. After lunch and exploration time at the dunes, we continue to the charming town of Tsetserleg, nestled in the mountains.

  • Morning briefing and motorcycle handover
  • Explore the Elsen Tasarkhai sand dunes
  • Camel riding opportunity (optional)
  • Overnight in comfortable ger camp near Tsetserleg
  • Welcome dinner with traditional Mongolian cuisine
2

Tsetserleg ‚Üí Terkhiin Tsagaan Lake ‚Üí Khorgo

200 km

Today we ride through stunning mountain scenery to reach the breathtaking Terkhiin Tsagaan Lake (White Lake). This freshwater lake is surrounded by extinct volcanoes and lush meadows, offering some of the most picturesque views in Mongolia. We'll spend time exploring the lake area and continue to Khorgo, an extinct volcano with unique lava formations.

  • Morning ride through mountainous terrain
  • Picnic lunch with panoramic views of White Lake
  • Explore the Khorgo volcano crater
  • Evening fishing opportunity (seasonal)
  • Overnight in lakeside ger camp
3

Khorgo ‚Üí Khatgal (Khuvsgul Lake)

300 km

Our longest riding day takes us north through diverse landscapes to reach Mongolia's crown jewel - the crystal-clear Khuvsgul Lake. This sacred lake contains about 1% of the world's fresh water and is surrounded by mountains and forests. We arrive at the small town of Khatgal, the gateway to the lake.

  • Full day of varied riding terrain
  • Visit traditional nomadic families en route
  • First glimpse of Mongolia's "Blue Pearl" - Khuvsgul Lake
  • Evening bonfire by the lakeshore
  • Overnight in comfortable lakeside cabins
4

Khatgal (Rest Day)

Local exploration

Today is a well-deserved rest day at Khuvsgul Lake. Take time to relax, enjoy the pristine nature, or engage in various optional activities. The area offers beautiful hiking trails through forests, boat rides on the crystal-clear water, and opportunities to interact with local Tsaatan reindeer herders who inhabit the region.

  • Free morning to rest and rejuvenate
  • Optional boat trip on Khuvsgul Lake
  • Hiking opportunities in the surrounding forests
  • Visit to local Tsaatan reindeer herder families (optional)
  • Second night in lakeside accommodation
5

Khatgal ‚Üí Khankh (Northern shore)

150 km

Today we ride along the western shore of Khuvsgul Lake, following scenic trails with panoramic vistas of the water and surrounding mountains. The ride takes us to Khankh, a remote settlement near the Russian border. This less-visited part of the lake offers unspoiled natural beauty and tranquility.

  • Breathtaking shoreline riding with lake views
  • Stops at pristine beaches for swimming (summer)
  • Wildlife spotting opportunities (deer, rare birds)
  • Visit to ancient shamanic rock inscriptions
  • Overnight in basic but comfortable ger camp
6

Khankh ‚Üí Renchinlkhumbe ‚Üí Ulaan-Uul

250 km

Today we venture into the remote Darkhad Depression, home to some of Mongolia's most isolated communities. We'll ride through the challenging but beautiful terrain to the settlements of Renchinlkhumbe and Ulaan-Uul. This area is known for its traditional shamanic culture and pristine wilderness.

  • Technical riding through mountain passes
  • Visit to a local shaman (if available)
  • Lunch with nomadic family in Renchinlkhumbe
  • Cross several river fords (support vehicle assistance)
  • Overnight in community-run guesthouse
7

Ulaan-Uul ‚Üí Bulgan Province

270 km

Leaving the remote northern regions behind, we begin our journey southward through the scenic landscapes of Bulgan Province. This area is characterized by rolling hills, forested mountains, and open grasslands where nomadic herders tend to their animals. We'll stay in a comfortable ger camp in the heart of Bulgan.

  • Forest trail riding through pine and larch forests
  • Visit to ancient Buddhist monastery ruins
  • Traditional archery demonstration
  • Scenic picnic lunch in mountain meadow
  • Overnight in tourist ger camp with hot showers
8

Bulgan ‚Üí Khustai National Park

250 km

Today's ride brings us to Khustai National Park, home to the successfully reintroduced Przewalski's wild horses (takhi). These are the last truly wild horses on Earth, and the park offers an excellent opportunity to observe them in their natural habitat. The diverse steppe ecosystem also supports many other wildlife species.

  • Morning ride with mountain backdrop
  • Wildlife spotting in Khustai National Park
  • Guided tour with park ranger to find wild horses
  • Traditional barbecue dinner (khorkhog)
  • Final night camping experience with farewell celebration
9

Khustai National Park ‚Üí Ulaanbaatar

100 km

Our final day of riding takes us back to Ulaanbaatar. We'll have a relaxed morning at Khustai with one last chance to observe wildlife before taking a leisurely ride back to the capital. Upon arrival, we'll return the motorcycles, check into our hotel, and celebrate our amazing journey with a special farewell dinner in the city.

  • Morning wildlife viewing opportunity
  • Easy ride back to Ulaanbaatar
  • Return of motorcycles and equipment
  • Free time for souvenir shopping
  • Farewell dinner at top Mongolian restaurant
